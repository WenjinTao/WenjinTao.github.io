## What I'm Working On

Currently, I am:

- Leading product work focused on manufacturing automation and intelligent systems
- Exploring agentic AI, digital twins, and workflow tools for industrial settings
- Looking for better ways to connect advanced technology with actual operator and factory needs

## Past Experience

In 2012, I co-founded MeoryTech, where we designed and built desktop 3D printers and supporting software.
We worked with research institutes and consulting firms, which gave me an early education in product design,
execution, and the difference between a clever prototype and a system people will pay to use.

That experience shaped how I think about scope, adoption, and cross-functional work to this day.

## Research Background

I earned a PhD in Mechanical Engineering from Missouri University of Science and Technology. My research
centered on worker-centered intelligent manufacturing, including multimodal sensing, human activity
recognition, and intelligent systems that support people on the factory floor.

## Outside of Work

Outside of work, I like exploring ideas that cross disciplines. Lately that has included music production,
writing, and following the odd connections between engineering, culture, and everyday life.
