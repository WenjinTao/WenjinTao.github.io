As automation transforms industries, product managers working in robotics and manufacturing must develop a unique skill set that bridges traditional software product management with hardware-software system thinking.

## The Hardware-Software Divide

Software PMs are accustomed to rapid iteration cycles, A/B testing, and continuous deployment. In robotics, the feedback loops are longer, the cost of errors is higher, and the integration challenges are more complex.

## Key Skills for Robotics PMs

- Understanding of control systems and sensor technologies
- Familiarity with safety standards and compliance requirements
- Ability to manage hardware-software co-development timelines
- Experience with field deployment and maintenance logistics

## Bridging the Gap

The most effective product leaders in robotics are those who can translate between engineering disciplines, maintain a user-centered perspective, and make pragmatic tradeoffs between innovation speed and system reliability.
