## Overview

In response to the COVID-19 pandemic ventilator shortage, designed and prototyped a low-cost emergency ventilator suitable for rapid manufacturing using widely available components.

## Key Contributions

- Mechanical design optimized for 3D printing and off-the-shelf parts
- Embedded control system for pressure and volume regulation
- Safety monitoring and alarm systems
- Documentation for distributed manufacturing
