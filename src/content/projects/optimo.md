## Overview

OPTIMO is an AI-driven platform that analyzes human operations on the factory floor using computer vision techniques including pose estimation and activity recognition to optimize workflows and improve ergonomics.

## Key Contributions

- Real-time human pose estimation for operator tracking
- Activity recognition for automated time studies
- Ergonomic risk assessment using biomechanical models
- Optimization recommendations for workstation design
